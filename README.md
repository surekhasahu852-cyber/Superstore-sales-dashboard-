Superstore sales dashboard 

PURPOSE 
Th superstore sales dashboard was created to analyse the sales performance of the store. We also provide insights into revenue,profit, customer segments, product performance and regional profitability.It helps business to identify opportunities.It also helps business to grow and find an opportunity.

TECH STACK 
Microsoft excel 
Pivot tables 
Pivot charts 
Slicers 
Data cleaning and formatting 
Dashboard design 

Data source 
Sample superstore dataset(kaggle)

HIGHLIGHTS 
Interactive dashboard with slicers for month, segment and region.
KPI cards displaying total sales, profit, quantity sold and orders.
Monthly sales trend analysis (2014-2017).
sales distribution across categories and customer segments.
Regional profit comparison.
